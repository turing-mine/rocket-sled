RocketSled allows you to take photos from your laptop or phone and instantly save them to the cloud, also saving local environmental data at the time the photo is taken.
lat and lon is the geo-location of this photo; the location metadata was retrieved from the photo via this app.  This app can also store other info along with the photo, such as magnetic bearing, temperature and atmospheric pressure, depending on your mobile device's capabilities.
Once the photos and environmental data are stored, they can be uploaded to a cloud service, such as Google Photos.  This makes it an ideal field tool for first responders and forestry personnel, collecting field data just by taking photos.
